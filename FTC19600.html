About FIRST Tech Challenge

The FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics competition program for middle and high school students, part of the FIRST organization's initiatives. It challenges teams to design, build, and program a robot to complete various tasks in an annual game, promoting hands-on STEM learning, problem-solving, and teamwork. FTC participants use a standard set of components to construct their robots, fostering creativity and innovation, and they also keep engineering notebooks and participate in interviews, developing important communication and presentation skills. FTC aims to inspire a passion for science and technology among young minds, preparing them for future careers in STEM fields while emphasizing values like gracious professionalism and collaboration.
